    The various terms used to describe the different (and sometimes overlapping) geographical and political areas of the islands of Great Britain, Ireland and surrounding islands are often a source of confusion, partly owing to the similarity between some of the actual words used, but also because they are often used loosely.  The purpose of this article is to explain the meanings of and inter-relationships among those terms. In brief, the main terms and their simple explanations are as follows.
