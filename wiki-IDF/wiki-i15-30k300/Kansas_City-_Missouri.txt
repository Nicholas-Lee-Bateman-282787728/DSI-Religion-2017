Situated opposite Kansas City, Kansas, the city was the location of several battles during the Civil War, including the Battle of Westport.  The city is well known for its contributions to the musical styles of jazz and blues as well as to cuisine (Kansas City-style barbecue).  Kansas City, Missouri, is often abbreviated as "KCMO", or simply "KC" (both abbreviations often refer to the metro area). It is officially nicknamed the City of Fountains, with over 200 examples, the city claims to have second most in the world, just behind Rome. The city also has more boulevards than any city except Paris and has often been called "Paris on the Plains."
